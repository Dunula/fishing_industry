Welcome to Fishing Industry

The fishing industry includes any industry or activity concerned with taking, culturing, processing, preserving, storing, transporting, marketing or selling fish or fish products.

The fishing industry in the United States was valued at around 11.5 billion U.S. dollars in 2022, which continues the recovery recorded in the previous year.
