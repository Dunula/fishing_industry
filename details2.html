Cod


* This flaky white fish is a great source of phosphorus, niacin, and vitamin B-12. A 3-ounce cooked portion contains 15 to 20 grams of protein.

* Cod is a low-fat source of protein, making it an excellent choice for people who would like to reduce their fat intake and improve their heart health. Cod also contains a significant amount of iodine, which is an important mineral for thyroid function.

* Cod – cod has a mild flavor that's slightly sweet like tilapia. People love cod because of its soft, flaky texture. It's firmer than tilapia so preparing it at home is easier if you don't have experience with seafood.
